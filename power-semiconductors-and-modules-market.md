<p><p>Power semiconductors and modules market consists of various types including MOSFETs, discrete IGBTs, IGBT modules, and others. MOSFETs are metal-oxide-semiconductor field-effect transistors used in power electronics applications. Discrete IGBTs are insulated gate bipolar transistors that are used for switching and amplifying electronic signals. IGBT modules combine the advantages of both MOSFETs and IGBTs. The "other" market in power semiconductors and modules includes emerging technologies and niche products that cater to specific industry needs.</p></p>
