<p><p>Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM) equipment market consists of two main types: Coarse Wavelength Division Multiplexing (CWDM) Equipment and Dense Wavelength Division Multiplexing (DWDM) Equipment. CWDM equipment allows multiple signals to be transmitted over different wavelengths on the same fiber optic cable with wider spacing between channels. On the other hand, DWDM equipment enables more channels to be transmitted at tighter spacing, making it suitable for long-distance high capacity data transmission. Both CWDM and DWDM equipment play a crucial role in expanding network capacity and improving data transmission efficiency.</p></p>
