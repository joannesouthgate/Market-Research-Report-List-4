<p><p>Industrial Smart Autonomous Guided Vehicles (AGVs) are categorized based on their guidance systems. Laser Guidance AGVs use lasers to navigate and avoid obstacles. Magnetic Guidance AGVs follow magnetic tape or wires installed in the facility. Infrared Guidance AGVs use infrared signals for navigation. Optical Guided AGVs rely on cameras and sensors for guidance. Other types of AGVs may include those guided by radio frequency identification (RFID) or vision-based systems. Each type offers unique benefits and functionalities tailored to specific industrial requirements.</p></p>
