<p><strong>The Aquaponics Farming Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Substrate Cultivation</li><li>Deep-Water Raft Cultivation</li><li>Nutrient Film Pipeline Cultivation</li><li>Aerosol Cultivation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aquaponics farming market includes various types of cultivation methods such as substrate cultivation, deep-water raft cultivation, nutrient film pipeline cultivation, and aerosol cultivation. Substrate cultivation involves growing plants in a medium like gravel or rock wool. Deep-water raft cultivation involves floating rafts with plants on top of a nutrient-rich water solution. Nutrient film pipeline cultivation utilizes a shallow stream of nutrient-rich water flowing over plant roots. Aerosol cultivation involves misting plant roots with a nutrient solution. Each method offers different benefits and challenges for aquaponics farming.</p></p>
