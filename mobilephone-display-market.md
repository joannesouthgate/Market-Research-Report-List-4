<p><ul><li>IPS</li><li>ASV</li><li>TFT</li><li>OLED</li><li>STN</li><li>CSTN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobilephone display market consists of various types including IPS, ASV, TFT, OLED, STN, and CSTN. IPS (In-Plane Switching) displays offer wide viewing angles and accurate colors. ASV (Advanced Super View) displays provide good image quality even in bright light conditions. TFT (Thin-Film Transistor) displays offer fast response times and high refresh rates. OLED (Organic Light-Emitting Diode) displays provide vivid colors and deep blacks. STN (Super Twisted Nematic) displays offer low power consumption. CSTN (Color Super Twisted Nematic) displays provide improved color accuracy compared to STN.</p></p>
