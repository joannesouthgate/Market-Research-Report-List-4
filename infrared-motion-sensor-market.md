<p><ul><li>Passive Infrared Sensor</li><li>Imaging Infrared Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Infrared Motion Sensor market consists of two main types: Passive Infrared Sensor and Imaging Infrared Sensor. Passive Infrared Sensors detect infrared energy emitted by or reflected from a moving object, triggering a response such as turning on a light. Imaging Infrared Sensors, on the other hand, create visual representations of infrared radiation, allowing for more detailed analysis of moving objects. Both types of sensors are widely used in security systems, automatic lighting, and other applications that require motion detection.</p></p>
