<p><strong>The GNSS Receivers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Frequency GNSS Receiver</li><li>Multi-GNSS Receiver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A single-frequency GNSS receiver is a device that can only receive signals from one GNSS system, such as GPS. On the other hand, a multi-GNSS receiver is capable of receiving signals from multiple GNSS systems simultaneously, including GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, and BeiDou. Multi-GNSS receivers offer more accurate and reliable positioning data compared to single-frequency GNSS receivers. Additionally, they have better coverage and can provide better performance in challenging environments with obstructed or limited sky view.</p></p>
