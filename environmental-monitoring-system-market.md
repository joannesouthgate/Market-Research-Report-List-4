<p><p>Environmental Monitoring System Market includes two main types: Portable and Stationary. Portable systems are lightweight, easy to carry, and can be moved from one location to another for on-the-spot monitoring. They are preferred for temporary monitoring needs or fieldwork. On the other hand, stationary systems are fixed in one location and continuously monitor environmental parameters. They are commonly used in industrial settings, research facilities, or government monitoring stations. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and health of our environment.</p></p>
