<p><p>The croissant market is divided into two main types: shelf-stable and frozen croissants. In contrast, frozen croissants are preserved through freezing, ensuring freshness and flavor until thawed and baked. This type appeals to bakeries and restaurants that prefer to bake on demand, offering freshly baked products while managing inventory effectively. Both segments cater to evolving consumer preferences for quality and convenience.</p></p>
