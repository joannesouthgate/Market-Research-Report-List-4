<p><p>Energy efficient windows are designed to help reduce heat loss and increase energy efficiency in buildings. Double glazing windows consist of two panes of glass with a layer of gas between them, providing better insulation compared to single-pane windows. Triple Low-E glazing takes this a step further by adding a low-emissivity coating to the glass, further reducing heat transfer. Both types of windows are popular in the market for their energy-saving properties and ability to improve overall comfort and energy savings in homes and buildings.</p></p>
