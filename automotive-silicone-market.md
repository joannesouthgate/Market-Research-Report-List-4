<p><strong>The Automotive Silicone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Temperature Vulcanised (HTV)</li><li>Room Temperature Vulcanised (RTV)</li><li>Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR)</li><li>Fluorosilicone Rubber (FSR)</li><li>High Consistency Silicone Rubber (HCR)</li><li>Silicone Gels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive silicone market primarily consists of five types: High Temperature Vulcanised (HTV), Room Temperature Vulcanised (RTV), Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR), Fluorosilicone Rubber (FSR), and High Consistency Silicone Rubber (HCR). HTV is heat-cured and used for high temperature applications, RTV cures at room temperature, LSR is liquid form suitable for injection molding, FSR is resistant to fuel and oil, and HCR is a solid form used in extrusion and compression molding. Additionally, silicone gels are also used in automotive applications for their flexibility and resistance to extreme temperatures.</p></p>
