<p><p>The floorboard adhesive market consists of various types, each tailored for specific applications. Epoxy adhesives provide strong bonding and resistance to moisture, making them suitable for high-traffic areas. Urethane adhesives offer flexibility and durability, ideal for wood and laminate floors. Acrylic adhesives are known for their fast curing and non-toxic properties, suitable for a wide range of materials. Vinyl adhesives are specifically formulated for vinyl flooring, ensuring a strong bond. Other adhesives might include animal-based or specialized industrial solutions, catering to unique flooring needs.</p></p>
