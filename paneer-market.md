<p><ul><li>Soft Paneer</li><li>Semi-soft Paneer</li><li>Medium-hard Paneer</li><li>Hard Paneer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paneer market is categorized into four primary types based on texture and firmness. Soft paneer, often creamy and easily spreadable, is popular for use in dishes like paneer tikka. Semi-soft paneer has a firmer texture, suitable for grilling and baking. Medium-hard paneer holds its shape well, making it ideal for cooking in curries. Hard paneer, with its dense consistency, is often shredded or grated for use in various culinary applications. Each type caters to specific culinary needs and consumer preferences.</p></p>
