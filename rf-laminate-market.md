<p><strong>The RF Laminate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Under 3 Dk (Dielectric Constant)</li><li>3 to 4 Dk (Dielectric Constant)</li><li>4 to 5 Dk (Dielectric Constant)</li><li>Greater than 5 Dk (Dielectric Constant)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF laminate market types are differentiated based on the dielectric constant (Dk) of the material. Under 3 Dk laminates are low-loss materials ideal for high-frequency applications. 3 to 4 Dk laminates offer a balance between cost efficiency and performance. 4 to 5 Dk laminates provide increased signal integrity and impedance control. Laminates with a dielectric constant greater than 5 Dk are suitable for high-speed digital applications requiring minimal signal loss. Each type caters to specific requirements in the RF industry based on their Dk characteristics.</p></p>
