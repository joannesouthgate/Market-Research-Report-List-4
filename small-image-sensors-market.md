<p><p>The small image sensor market primarily comprises Vidicon image sensors and solid-state image sensors. Vidicon sensors, based on an analog vacuum tube technology, are used in older TV cameras and medical imaging, offering good sensitivity but less durability. Solid-state image sensors, such as CMOS and CCD, dominate the market due to their compact size, low power consumption, and higher image quality. They are widely used in digital cameras, smartphones, and various imaging applications, driving advancements in imaging technologies.</p></p>
