<p><p>Sputtering equipment cathode market types include linear and circular cathodes. Linear cathodes are long and narrow, providing a linear deposition area for coating materials onto substrates. Circular cathodes, on the other hand, have a circular shape, offering a more uniform coating distribution. Linear cathodes are commonly used for larger substrates with straight edges, while circular cathodes are ideal for coating smaller, curved surfaces. Both types play a crucial role in sputtering equipment for various industrial applications.</p></p>
