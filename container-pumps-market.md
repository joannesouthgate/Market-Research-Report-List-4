<p><p>Container pumps market includes various types such as canister pumps, siphon pumps, hand pumps, and foot pumps. Canister pumps are convenient for dispensing liquids from containers like fuel cans. Siphon pumps are used for transferring liquids without the need for electricity or manual pumping. Hand pumps are manually operated pumps for dispensing liquids like water or fuel. Foot pumps are foot-operated pumps commonly used in inflating items like air mattresses or inflatable boats. Each type serves specific purposes in the market for efficient liquid handling.</p></p>
