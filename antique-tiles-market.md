<p><p>The antique tiles market can be divided into two main types: ceramics and natural stone. Ceramics refer to tiles made from clay that are typically glazed and fired in a kiln. These tiles come in a wide range of colors, patterns, and designs. Natural stone tiles, on the other hand, are made from materials such as marble, granite, limestone, or slate. These tiles offer a more organic and unique look, with variations in color and veining that add character to any space. Both ceramics and natural stone tiles are sought after for their durability, timeless appeal, and ability to elevate the aesthetic of any interior or exterior setting.</p></p>
