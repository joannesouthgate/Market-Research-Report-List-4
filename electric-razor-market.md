<p><p>The electric razor market can be broadly categorized into two types: foil and rotary. Foil razors have a thin layer of metal covering the blades, which help to capture and cut hair close to the skin for a smooth shave. On the other hand, rotary razors have circular blades that move in a circular motion, making them ideal for cutting longer hairs and adapting to the contours of the face. Both types of razors offer unique features and cater to different preferences of consumers.</p></p>
