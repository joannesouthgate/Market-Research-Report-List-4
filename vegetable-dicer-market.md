<p><p>Vegetable dicer market is divided into two main types based on their cutting method: Vertical Type and Horizontal Type. Vertical type vegetable dicers are designed to cut vegetables in a downward motion, while horizontal type vegetable dicers cut vegetables from a side-to-side motion. Vertical type dicers are typically more compact and easier to use for smaller quantities of vegetables, while horizontal type dicers are better suited for larger quantities and more heavy-duty chopping. Both types offer efficient and convenient ways to dice vegetables for cooking.</p></p>
