<p><p>Dental materials market primarily consists of four types - ceramic, amalgam, composite, and others. Ceramic materials are known for their natural appearance and durability, making them popular for crowns and veneers. Amalgam is a cost-effective option for fillings but has become less common due to concerns about mercury content. Composite materials provide excellent aesthetics and are suitable for a variety of restorations.
