<p><strong>The Flat Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronically-steered Antenna</li><li>Mechanically-steered Antenna</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flat antennas market is primarily divided into electronically-steered and mechanically-steered antennas. Electronically-steered antennas utilize electronic controls to adjust the beam direction without moving parts, offering faster response and improved adaptability, commonly used in applications like satellite communications and radar systems. In contrast, mechanically-steered antennas rely on physical movement to change direction, which can be more straightforward but often slower. Both types cater to various industries, including telecommunications, aerospace, and defense, addressing diverse user needs for communication and surveillance.</p></p>
