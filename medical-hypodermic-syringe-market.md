<p><p>Medical hypodermic syringe market can be categorized into different types based on the tip angle of the needle. Short bevel syringes have a tip angle ranging from 30-45°, which is suitable for accessing veins and arteries. Long bevel syringes, on the other hand, have a tip angle of 12-15°, making them ideal for intradermal injections. Other types of syringes in the market include safety syringes with features like retractable needles or needle shields to prevent accidental needlestick injuries. These different types cater to specific medical needs and preferences.</p></p>
