<p><p>Polypropylene film capacitors are categorized into two main types: plain polypropylene film capacitors and metallised polypropylene film capacitors. Plain polypropylene film capacitors are made of a single layer of polypropylene film without any metal coating, offering high insulation resistance and excellent frequency characteristics. On the other hand, metallised polypropylene film capacitors have a thin metal layer deposited on the film to increase their capacitance and efficiency. Both types are widely used in various electronic applications due to their reliability and performance.</p></p>
