<p><p>Potentiometers are variable resistors used to adjust electrical resistance in a circuit. The two main types are rotary potentiometers and linear potentiometers. Rotary potentiometers have a circular dial that can be turned to change the resistance, commonly used in audio equipment and control knobs. Linear potentiometers have a slider that moves along a straight track to adjust resistance, often used in industrial machinery and motion control systems. Both types serve the same function of providing precise resistance adjustment in various applications.</p></p>
