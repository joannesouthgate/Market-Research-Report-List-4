<p><p>Thermal spray coating system market includes various types such as Flame Spray, Electric Arc Wire Spray, Plasma Spray, and others. Flame spray involves melting materials in a flame and spraying them onto a surface. Electric Arc Wire Spray uses an electric arc to melt a wire and propel it onto a surface. Plasma Spray involves melting materials with a high-temperature plasma and coating a surface with the molten droplets. Other types of thermal spray coating systems may involve different methods of melting and spraying materials onto surfaces.</p></p>
