<p><ul><li>Standalone</li><li>In-Band</li><li>Guard Band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>NB IoT Chipset market types can be categorized into Standalone, In-Band, and Guard Band. Standalone NB IoT Chipsets operate independently without relying on existing cellular networks, offering enhanced coverage and improved performance. In-Band NB IoT Chipsets operate within existing LTE spectrum, sharing resources with other cellular technologies. Guard Band NB IoT Chipsets utilize unused spectrum between LTE channels, minimizing interference and maximizing efficiency. Each market type serves different purposes and caters to specific needs within the IoT ecosystem.</p></p>
