<p><p>Thick film resistor networks are essential components in various electronic applications, available primarily in two market types: Single-In-Line (SIL) and Dual-In-Line (DIL) configurations. SIL resistor networks feature a single row of resistors, allowing for compact designs with reduced footprint, suitable for space-constrained applications. On the other hand, DIL resistor networks have two rows of resistors, facilitating easier integration into circuit boards and providing better handling of higher power ratings. Both types offer customizable resistance values and are used in diverse electronic systems.</p></p>
