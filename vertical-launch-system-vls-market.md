<p><ul><li>Hot Launch</li><li>Cold Launch</li><li>Concentric Canister Launch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vertical Launch System (VLS) market consists of three main types: Hot Launch, Cold Launch, and Concentric Canister Launch. Hot Launch systems ignite the missile inside the canister, allowing for quick missile deployment. Cold Launch systems eject the missile from the canister before igniting it, reducing the chances of damage to the launch platform. Concentric Canister Launch systems use a concentric canister design to launch missiles, providing efficient use of space and easy reload capabilities. Each type caters to different requirements and preferences in the defense industry.</p></p>
