<p><p>Thermoplastic PUR Hot Melts Adhesives are solid at room temperature and can be melted and applied when heated. They provide strong bonding properties and are commonly used in packaging, woodworking, and assembly applications. On the other hand, Reactive PUR Hot Melts Adhesives undergo a chemical reaction when exposed to moisture or heat, which helps in curing and forming a durable bond. These adhesives are often used in demanding applications where high strength and resistance to heat and chemicals are required.</p></p>
