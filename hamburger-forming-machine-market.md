<p><p>Hamburger forming machines are available in two main types: double head forming machines and single head forming machines. Double head forming machines are equipped with two forming heads, allowing them to produce two hamburger patties simultaneously. This type of machine is ideal for high-volume production. On the other hand, single head forming machines have only one forming head and are suitable for lower volume production. Both types of machines are designed to efficiently and consistently shape ground meat into uniform hamburger patties.</p></p>
