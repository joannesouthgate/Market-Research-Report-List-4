<p><p>Well intervention services can be categorized into three main types: light intervention, medium intervention, and heavy intervention. Light intervention involves less complex operations such as wireline interventions and hydraulic workovers. Medium intervention includes tasks like coiled tubing interventions and snubbing operations, requiring a moderate level of complexity. Heavy intervention services are the most complex and involve activities like hydraulic fracturing and well abandonment. Each type of intervention service is tailored to specific well conditions and requirements to ensure efficient and effective well maintenance and optimization.</p></p>
