<p><p>Smartphone screens can be categorized into different types based on the technology used. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) screens are commonly used and offer good color accuracy. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diodes) screens provide vibrant colors and deep blacks. AMOLED (Active Matrix Organic Light Emitting Diodes) screens are a variation of OLED screens with improved energy efficiency. Other types of screens include IPS (In-Plane Switching) and TFT (Thin Film Transistor). Each type has its own advantages and cater to different needs of users in the smartphone market.</p></p>
