<p><p>Commercial Smoke Alarm Market offers three types of smoke alarms: </p><p>1. Photoelectric Smoke Alarm utilizes a beam of light to detect smoke particles, ideal for detecting slow, smoldering fires. </p><p>2. Ionization Smoke Alarm uses a small amount of radioactive material to ionize the air and detect smoke particles, best for fast-flaming fires. </p><p>3. Dual Sensor Smoke Alarm combines both photoelectric and ionization technologies for comprehensive fire detection. Each type has its strengths in different types of fires, providing options for various commercial environments.</p></p>
