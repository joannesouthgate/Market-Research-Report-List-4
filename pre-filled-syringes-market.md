<p><strong>The Pre-Filled Syringes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glass Pre-Filled Syringes</li><li>Plastic Pre-Filled Syringes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pre-Filled Syringes Market includes two main types: Glass Pre-Filled Syringes and Plastic Pre-Filled Syringes. Glass syringes are made of borosilicate glass, which is inert and provides good chemical resistance. They are commonly used for biologics and vaccines. Plastic syringes are made of polymer materials such as cyclic olefin copolymer (COC) or cyclic olefin polymer (COP). They are lightweight, break-resistant, and offer more flexibility in design. Both types are widely used in the pharmaceutical and biotechnology industries for convenient drug delivery.</p></p>
