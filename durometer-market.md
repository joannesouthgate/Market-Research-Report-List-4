<p><p>Durometer market can be classified into various types based on the method used to measure the hardness of materials. Brinell Durometer measures hardness by indenting a material with a hardened steel ball. Vickers Durometer uses a diamond pyramid to measure hardness. Rockwell Durometer measures hardness by indenting the material with a cone-shaped tool. Other types of Durometers include Shore Durometer, Leeb Durometer, and Barcol Durometer, each using different methods to determine material hardness.</p></p>
