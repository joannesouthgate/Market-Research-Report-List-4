<p><p>Linear Queue Management System Market Types include Structured Queue, Unstructured Queue, Kiosk-Based Queue, and Moving Queue. Structured Queue follows a first-come-first-served order with clearly defined lines. Unstructured Queue allows customers to roam freely while waiting for their turn. Kiosk-Based Queue enables customers to check themselves in and monitor their queue status. Moving Queue system uses digital displays to inform customers of their position in the queue, allowing them to move around until their turn arrives. Each market type caters to different customer preferences and operational needs.</p></p>
