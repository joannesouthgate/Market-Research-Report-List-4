<p><p>Disk lasers come in three different power categories: low power, mid power, and high power. Low power disk lasers are typically used for welding, cutting, and marking applications that require precision and accuracy. Mid power disk lasers are often used for metal processing, such as cutting thicker materials and higher-speed welding. High power disk lasers are suitable for heavy-duty industrial applications that require high cutting speeds and deep penetration, such as in the automotive, aerospace, and shipbuilding industries.</p></p>
