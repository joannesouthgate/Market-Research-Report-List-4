<p><p>Water quality sensors are used to monitor various parameters in water to ensure safety and quality. Different types of sensors serve different purposes in measuring water quality. Chlorine residual sensors measure the amount of chlorine in water for disinfection purposes. TOC sensors detect the total organic carbon content in water. Turbidity sensors measure the cloudiness or haziness of water. Conductivity sensors measure the ability of water to conduct electricity. pH sensors measure the acidity or alkalinity of water. ORP sensors measure the oxidation-reduction potential of water.</p></p>
