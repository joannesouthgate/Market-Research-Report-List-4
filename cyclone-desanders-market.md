<p><p>Cyclone desanders come in two main market types: Monoblock and Multi-body Parallel. Monoblock desanders consist of a single cyclonic unit, designed for smaller scale applications and easier installation. On the other hand, Multi-body Parallel desanders combine multiple cyclonic units in parallel, offering higher processing capacity for larger-scale operations. Both types are effective in separating solid particles from liquids in industries such as oil and gas, mining, and wastewater treatment. The choice between the two types depends on the specific needs and scale of the application.</p></p>
