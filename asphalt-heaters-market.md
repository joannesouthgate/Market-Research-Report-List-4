<p><p>Asphalt heaters come in various types including infrared heating, steam heating, electric heating, and others. Infrared heating uses radiation to heat the asphalt surface quickly and efficiently. Steam heating involves circulating steam through coils to heat the asphalt. Electric heating utilizes electric elements to provide heat. Other types of asphalt heaters may include propane heating or diesel heating. Each type of heater offers its own advantages and is used in different applications based on factors such as cost, efficiency, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
