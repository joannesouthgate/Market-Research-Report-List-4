<p><p>Water screen projection is a technology that involves shooting high-powered video projectors onto a thin, permeable screen made of water vapor or water droplets. This technology is often used in outdoor events such as concerts, theme parks, and multimedia shows.</p><p>The water screen projection market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.5% during the forecast period.
