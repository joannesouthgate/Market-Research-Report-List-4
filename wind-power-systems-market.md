<p><p>The Wind Power Systems market can be categorized into two main types: On-Grid and Off-Grid. On-Grid systems are connected to the electricity grid and feed power directly into it, allowing for excess electricity to be sold back to the grid. Off-Grid systems, on the other hand, are standalone systems that are not connected to the grid and typically used in remote areas or places where access to the grid is difficult. Both systems play a crucial role in harnessing wind energy for power generation.</p></p>
