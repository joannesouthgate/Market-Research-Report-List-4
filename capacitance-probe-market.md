<p><p>A Capacitance Probe is a sensor device that measures the level of material in a container by detecting changes in capacitance as the material level changes. It consists of a metal probe and a reference ground probe, both of which create an electric field. As the material level rises or falls, the capacitance between the two probes changes, allowing for precise level measurement.</p><p>The Capacitance Probe Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.00% during the forecast period.
