<p><p>Modular walk-in chambers are commonly categorized into two types: Panel walk-in chambers and Solid walk-in chambers. Panel walk-in chambers are constructed using panels that are usually pre-fabricated and assembled on-site. These chambers are more flexible and easier to customize based on specific requirements. On the other hand, solid walk-in chambers are built using solid materials such as metal or concrete, offering more stability and durability. Both types cater to various industries requiring controlled environmental conditions for testing and research purposes.</p></p>
