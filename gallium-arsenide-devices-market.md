<p><strong>The Gallium Arsenide Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SI GaAS</li><li>SC GaAs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gallium Arsenide Devices Market includes two main types - SI GaAs and SC GaAs. SI GaAs, or semi-insulating GaAs, is known for its high resistivity and is widely used in microwave devices for its low noise and high-power performance. On the other hand, SC GaAs, or semi-conducting GaAs, is favored for its high electron mobility, making it suitable for high-frequency applications such as optoelectronic devices. Both types play a crucial role in the semiconductor industry, catering to different needs and specifications.</p></p>
