<p><p>Silicone Rubber Waterproof Coatings come in two main types: Liquid and Dry formulations. Liquid Silicone Rubber Waterproof Coatings are applied as a liquid and then cure to form a flexible, waterproof barrier. Dry Silicone Rubber Waterproof Coatings, on the other hand, are pre-cured and come in sheets or rolls that can be applied directly to surfaces. Both types provide excellent waterproofing properties and are commonly used in construction, automotive, and electronic industries.</p></p>
