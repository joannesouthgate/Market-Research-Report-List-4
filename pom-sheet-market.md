<p><ul><li>Homo-polyoxymethylene</li><li>Co-polyoxymethylene</li></ul></p>
<p><p>POM sheet market is divided into two main types: homo-polyoxymethylene and co-polyoxymethylene. Homo-polyoxymethylene sheets are made from a single type of POM resin, offering high rigidity, strength, and resistance to moisture. They are ideal for applications requiring excellent dimensional stability. On the other hand, co-polyoxymethylene sheets are made from a blend of POM resins, providing improved chemical resistance and impact strength. They are commonly used in applications where a balance of properties is required. Both types cater to different market needs based on their specific characteristics.</p></p>
