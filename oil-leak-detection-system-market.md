<p><p>Passive Leak Detection Systems rely on sensors to continuously monitor for leaks in pipelines or storage tanks by detecting changes in pressure, temperature, or flow rate. Active Leak Detection Systems use technology such as infrared cameras or acoustic sensors to actively scan for leaks and quickly identify their location. Both systems play a crucial role in preventing environmental damage, ensuring safety, and minimizing financial losses in industries where oil leaks can have serious consequences.</p></p>
