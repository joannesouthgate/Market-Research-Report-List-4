<p><strong>The Application Specific IC ASIC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-Custom Design</li><li>Standard-Cell Based</li><li>Gate-Array Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Application Specific IC (ASIC) market includes three main types of designs: Full-Custom Design, Standard-Cell Based, and Gate-Array Based. </p><p>Full-Custom Design involves creating unique layouts for every component of the chip, offering high performance but at a higher cost. </p><p>Standard-Cell Based utilizes pre-designed cells that can be arranged to create custom circuits, balancing performance and cost. </p><p>Gate-Array Based involves a pre-defined grid of connected gates that can be customized by adding metal layers, offering lower cost but with reduced performance compared to full-custom design.</p></p>
