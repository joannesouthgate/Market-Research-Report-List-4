<p><ul><li>Bottled Water Cooler (BWC)</li><li>Point-of-Use (POU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water coolers and dispensers are available in two main types in the market. The first type is Bottled Water Cooler (BWC), where water is delivered in large bottles and placed on top of the cooler for dispensing. The second type is Point-of-Use (POU) coolers, which are directly connected to the water supply and do not require bottles. BWCs are popular for their convenience and portability, while POU coolers are preferred for their cost-effectiveness and eco-friendliness. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
