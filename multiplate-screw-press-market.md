<p><p>Multiplate Screw Press is a type of equipment used for solid-liquid separation in various industries such as food and beverage, wastewater treatment, and mining. The press utilizes a series of fixed and moving plates to effectively dewater sludge or other wet materials by squeezing them between the plates. This process results in the separation of solids and liquids, with the solids being discharged from the press and the liquid being collected for further processing or disposal.</p><p>The Multiplate Screw Press Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.9% during the forecast period.
