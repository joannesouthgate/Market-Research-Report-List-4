<p><p>Snake Spring Couplings are classified into two market types based on their stiffness: Transverse Stiffness Type and Variable Stiffness Type. Transverse Stiffness Type couplings provide a consistent level of stiffness across all angles, making them suitable for applications where uniform stiffness is required. On the other hand, Variable Stiffness Type couplings offer adjustable stiffness levels, enabling them to adapt to varying load conditions and provide more flexibility in overall system dynamics. Both types cater to different needs in mechanical systems requiring precise torque transmission and vibration damping.</p></p>
