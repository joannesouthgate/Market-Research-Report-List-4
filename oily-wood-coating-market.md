<p><p>Oily wood coatings can be categorized into different types based on their composition and method of application. Radiation curable coatings use ultraviolet light or electron beam radiation for curing. Other types of coatings may include water-based or powder coatings. Each type offers unique benefits and properties, catering to different application requirements in the wood coating market.</p></p>
