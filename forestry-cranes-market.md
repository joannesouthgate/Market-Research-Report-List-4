<p><p>Forestry cranes can be classified into three main types based on their boom configuration - A-Line cranes, L-Line cranes, and Z-Line cranes. A-Line cranes have a straight boom design that provides maximum reach and lifting capacity. L-Line cranes have a boom that forms an "L" shape, offering a balance between reach and maneuverability. Z-Line cranes have a "Z" shaped boom that allows for precise operations in tight spaces. Each type of crane has its own advantages and is suited for specific forestry applications.</p></p>
