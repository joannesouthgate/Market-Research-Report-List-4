<p><ul><li>Ester-based</li><li>Alcohol-based</li><li>Water-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ink binders are classified into three main types based on their composition: Ester-based, Alcohol-based, and Water-based binders. Ester-based binders are commonly used for solvent-based inks as they provide good adhesion and flexibility. Alcohol-based binders are known for their fast drying time and compatibility with various types of inks. Water-based binders are popular for their eco-friendly nature and low VOC emissions. Each type of binder serves different purposes and caters to specific ink formulations in the market.</p></p>
