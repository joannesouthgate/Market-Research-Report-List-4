<p><strong>The Smart Container Tracking System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>GPS (Global Positioning System)</li><li>Cellular</li><li>LoRa WAN (Long Range Wide Area Network)</li><li>BLE</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>GPS (Global Positioning System) technology utilizes satellites to track the location of containers in real-time, providing accurate and reliable data. Cellular tracking systems use mobile networks to transmit location information, offering widespread coverage and connectivity. LoRa WAN (Long Range Wide Area Network) technology is a low-power, long-range wireless protocol that enables cost-effective tracking over large distances. BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) technology allows for short-range tracking and communication between containers and devices. Other tracking systems may include RFID or satellite communication technologies.</p></p>
