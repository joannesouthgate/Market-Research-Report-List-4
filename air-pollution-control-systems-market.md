<p><p>Air pollution control systems encompass various technologies designed to reduce emissions from industrial processes. Scrubbers remove pollutants from exhaust gases by using liquid to trap particles and harmful gases. Catalytic converters facilitate chemical reactions that convert harmful pollutants into less harmful substances, primarily in vehicle emissions. Electrostatic precipitators use electrical charges to capture and remove particles from flue gases, making them highly effective in controlling fine particulate emissions. Together, these systems play a critical role in improving air quality and meeting regulatory standards.</p></p>
