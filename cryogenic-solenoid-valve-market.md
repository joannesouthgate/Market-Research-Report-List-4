<p><p>Cryogenic solenoid valves have various types including plunger valve, direct-acting plunger valve, and pilot-operated valves. Plunger valves operate by using a plunger to control the flow of cryogenic fluids. Direct-acting plunger valves directly control the flow without the need for additional mechanisms. Pilot-operated valves use a small valve to control the flow of a larger valve, allowing for precise regulation of cryogenic fluids. Each type of valve offers unique benefits and applications in the cryogenic solenoid valve market.</p></p>
