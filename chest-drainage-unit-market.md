<p><p>A Chest Drainage Unit (CDU) is a medical device designed to facilitate the drainage of fluids, air, or other substances from the pleural space in patients with conditions such as pneumothorax, pleural effusion, or post-surgical complications. The unit typically comprises a collection chamber, a one-way valve to prevent air from re-entering the pleural cavity, and tubing for connecting to the patient.</p><p>The Chest Drainage Unit Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.00% during the forecast period. Key drivers of this growth include an increase in respiratory diseases, rising surgical procedures, and advancements in medical technology. Recent trends indicate a shift toward more compact and portable designs, enabling easier patient mobility and improved comfort. Additionally, the integration of digital monitoring technologies is enhancing treatment precision and patient outcomes. Growing awareness regarding the management of chest-related ailments and the expansion of healthcare infrastructure, especially in emerging markets, further bolster the demand for CDUs.
