LC-MS is a powerful tool for identifying and quantifying biomarkers, offering high sensitivity and specificity. MS, on the other hand, is used for analyzing molecules based on their mass-to-charge ratio. Both technologies play a crucial role in pharmacodynamic biomarker testing, aiding in drug development and personalized medicine.</p></p>
