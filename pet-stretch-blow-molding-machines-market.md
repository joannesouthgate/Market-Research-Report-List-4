<p><ul><li>Single Step PET Stretch Blow Molding Machines</li><li>Two Step PET Stretch Blow Molding Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single step PET stretch blow molding machines are designed to produce bottles in a single process, combining both the preform molding and blowing stages. On the other hand, two-step PET stretch blow molding machines require separate preform molding and blowing processes. Single step machines are efficient for shorter production runs and quick changeovers, while two-step machines are more suitable for high-volume production with longer lead times. Both types of machines cater to different market segments based on production requirements and volumes.</p></p>
