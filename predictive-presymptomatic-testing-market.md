<p><p>Predictive presymptomatic testing is a growing market that includes three main types of testing: molecular testing, cytogenic testing, and biochemical testing. Molecular testing involves analyzing DNA to identify mutations that may predispose individuals to certain diseases. Cytogenic testing looks at the structure of chromosomes to detect genetic abnormalities. Biochemical testing measures levels of specific substances in the body that can indicate an increased risk of certain conditions. These different types of testing offer valuable insights into a person's genetic predispositions and can help guide preventative healthcare decisions.</p></p>
