<p><ul><li>Ground-based Broadband</li><li>Satellite Broadband</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ground-based broadband refers to in-flight internet services that are provided through terrestrial networks on the ground. This type of service uses cellular towers or ground antennas to connect the aircraft to the internet. On the other hand, satellite broadband market refers to in-flight internet services that use satellites in space to provide connectivity to the aircraft. This type of service offers wider coverage and faster speeds compared to ground-based broadband, but it is also more expensive to implement.</p></p>
