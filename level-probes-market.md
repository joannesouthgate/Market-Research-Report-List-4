<p><ul><li>Point Level Probes</li><li>Continuous Level Probes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Point Level Probes are used to detect the presence or absence of material at a specific point within a vessel or tank. They are typically used for high or low level alarms. Continuous Level Probes, on the other hand, provide real-time monitoring of material levels throughout the entire vessel or tank. They are used for precise measurement and control of inventory levels. Both types of probes are essential in industries such as manufacturing, food processing, and wastewater treatment for efficient and safe operation.</p></p>
