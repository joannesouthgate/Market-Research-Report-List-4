<p><p>Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) pads are used in semiconductor manufacturing for removing material during the polishing process. There are two main types of CMP pads: Hard CMP pads and Soft CMP pads. </p><p>Hard CMP pads are made of a firmer material and are used for high-pressure polishing applications, providing more aggressive material removal. Soft CMP pads, on the other hand, are made of a softer material and are used for low-pressure polishing applications, offering gentler material removal. Each type of pad is suited for different polishing needs in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
