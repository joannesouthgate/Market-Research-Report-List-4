<p><p>DLI Vaporizers are used in two main market types: DLI-CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) and DLI-ALD (Atomic Layer Deposition). In the DLI-CVD market, the vaporizers are used to deposit thin films of materials onto a substrate using chemical reactions in the gas phase. On the other hand, in the DLI-ALD market, the vaporizers are utilized for a precise layer-by-layer deposition process, where alternating reactant gases are introduced to create a film with atomic-scale control. Both markets rely on DLI vaporizers for their specific deposition needs.</p></p>
