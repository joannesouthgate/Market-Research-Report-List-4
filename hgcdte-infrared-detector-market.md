<p><strong>The HgCdTe Infrared Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LN2 Cooled</li><li>Thermoelectrically (TE) Cooled</li><li>Stirling Cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HgCdTe infrared detector market features three main cooling technologies: LN2 cooled, thermoelectrically (TE) cooled, and Stirling cooled. LN2 cooled detectors utilize liquid nitrogen for optimal cooling, enhancing sensitivity in low-light environments, commonly used in scientific applications. TE cooled detectors employ thermoelectric modules for compact, portable cooling, suitable for a range of applications. Stirling cooled detectors utilize a Stirling cycle engine for efficient cooling without the need for cryogens, offering enhanced performance in demanding environments. Each type provides unique benefits tailored for specific use cases.</p></p>
