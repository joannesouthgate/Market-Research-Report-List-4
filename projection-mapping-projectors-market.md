<p><p>Projection Mapping Projectors are mainly categorized into two types: DLP (Digital Light Processing) and LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) market. DLP projectors use tiny mirrors to create an image while LCD projectors utilize liquid crystal displays to produce images. DLP projectors are known for their high contrast ratio and deeper blacks, making them ideal for darker environments. On the other hand, LCD projectors are praised for their vibrant colors and sharper image quality. Both types of projectors have their own set of advantages and are widely used in various settings for projection mapping projects.</p></p>
