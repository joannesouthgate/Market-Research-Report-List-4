<p><p>Airport transceivers are communication devices used in airports for ground-to-air communication. The market is segmented into three types based on frequency bands: UHF, VHF, and HF airport transceivers. UHF transceivers operate in the ultra-high frequency band, VHF transceivers operate in the very high frequency band, and HF transceivers operate in the high frequency band. Each type of transceiver offers different ranges and capabilities to facilitate communication between ground staff, air traffic controllers, and pilots, ensuring safe and efficient airport operations.</p></p>
