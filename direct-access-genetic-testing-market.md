<p><p>Direct-access genetic testing market includes various types such as Diagnostic screening, Preimplantation genetic diagnosis (PGD), and Relationship testing. Diagnostic screening involves testing for specific genetic conditions or predispositions. PGD is used during in vitro fertilization to screen embryos for genetic disorders before implantation. Relationship testing is used to confirm biological relationships such as paternity or ancestry. Each type serves a unique purpose in providing individuals with valuable information about their genetic makeup and potential health risks.</p></p>
