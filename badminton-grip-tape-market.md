<p><p>Badminton grip tapes are popular in three primary types: rubber grip, towel grip, and over-grip. Rubber grip tapes provide a solid and secure grip, ideal for players who prefer a firmer hold on the racket. Towel grip tapes offer a comfortable and sweat-absorbing surface, preventing slippage during intense matches. Over-grip tapes are designed to be wrapped over existing grips to enhance comfort and control. Each type caters to different preferences and playing styles, making them essential accessories for badminton players of all levels.</p></p>
