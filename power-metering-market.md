<p><p>Power metering market consists of three main types: analog meters, digital meters, and smart meters. Analog meters are traditional meters that provide basic measurement of electricity usage. Digital meters offer more accurate readings and are equipped with digital displays for easier monitoring. Smart meters are the most advanced type, allowing for real-time monitoring and two-way communication between the meter and utility company. They enable features like time-of-use pricing and remote meter reading, making them the most efficient option for both consumers and utility providers.</p></p>
