<p><ul><li>Engineering BOM</li><li>Manufacturing BOM</li><li>Service BOM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>BOM software caters to three main market types - Engineering BOM, Manufacturing BOM, and Service BOM. Engineering BOM focuses on creating and managing the list of components required for designing a product. Manufacturing BOM deals with the list of materials needed for production processes. Service BOM involves maintaining an inventory of spare parts and components necessary for servicing products after they have been sold. Each market type serves a specific purpose in managing the bill of materials efficiently within different stages of a product's lifecycle.</p></p>
