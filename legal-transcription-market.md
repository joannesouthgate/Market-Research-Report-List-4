<p><p>The legal transcription market includes two main types: digital recording and electronic reporting. Digital recording involves the capture and storage of audio recordings of legal proceedings, which are then transcribed into written documents. Electronic reporting, on the other hand, utilizes real-time stenographic or voice writing technology to create instant transcripts of proceedings. Both types of transcription services are essential for legal professionals to accurately document and review court proceedings, depositions, and other important events in the legal field.</p></p>
