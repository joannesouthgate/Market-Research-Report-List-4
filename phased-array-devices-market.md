<p><ul><li>Electronic Scanning</li><li>Sector Scanning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Phased Array Devices Market Types consist of Electronic Scanning and Sector Scanning. Electronic Scanning involves electronically controlling the beam direction without moving physical components, allowing for rapid and precise beam steering. This type is widely used in radar systems and communication devices. Sector Scanning, on the other hand, involves mechanically scanning a fixed sector of space using a physically moving array. This type is commonly used in applications where a wide field of view is required, such as in security surveillance systems.</p></p>
