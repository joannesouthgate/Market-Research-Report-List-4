<p><p>The Analog to Digital Converter (ADC) market comprises various types designed for specific applications. Display ADCs focus on converting analog signals for visual output, commonly used in consumer electronics. Delta-sigma ADCs provide high-resolution conversion by oversampling, ideal for audio applications. Pipelined ADCs are known for their fast conversion speeds, suitable for high-performance systems. Dual slope ADCs offer high accuracy and noise rejection, making them ideal for precision measurement in industrial and scientific applications. Each type serves unique market needs driven by technological advancements.</p></p>
