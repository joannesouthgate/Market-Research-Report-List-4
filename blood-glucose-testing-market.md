<p><p>Blood Glucose Testing Market Types include Glucose Oxidase, Glucose Dehydrogenase, and Others. Glucose Oxidase is an enzyme that reacts with glucose to produce gluconic acid and hydrogen peroxide, which can be measured to determine blood glucose levels. Glucose Dehydrogenase is another type of enzyme that reacts with glucose to produce a signal that can be measured. The Others category includes alternative methods and technologies for measuring blood glucose levels, such as biosensors and optical sensors.</p></p>
