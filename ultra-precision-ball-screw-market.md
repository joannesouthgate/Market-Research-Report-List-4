<p><p>Ultra Precision Ball Screw Market includes two main types, Internal Circulation Ball Screw and External Circulation Ball Screw. Internal Circulation Ball Screws have the balls run along the grooves inside the nut and shaft, offering high rigidity and precision. External Circulation Ball Screws have the balls circulating external to the nut and shaft, providing higher load capacity and longer service life. Both types cater to different industrial needs and are essential components in various precision machinery and equipment.</p></p>
