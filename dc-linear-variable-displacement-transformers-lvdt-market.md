<p><p>DC Linear Variable Displacement Transformers (LVDT) market includes various types such as Free Core, Guided Core, Sprung Core, and Others. Free Core LVDTs have a movable core without any physical connection to the transformer body, providing high sensitivity and accuracy. Guided Core LVDTs have a core guided by a tube, offering better stability and ruggedness. Sprung Core LVDTs have a pre-loaded spring to maintain contact with the core, ensuring consistent measurements. Other types include special configurations designed for specific applications.</p></p>
