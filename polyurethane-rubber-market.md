<p><ul><li>Solid System</li><li>Liquid System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane rubber is a versatile material that can be classified into two main types: solid system and liquid system. Solid system polyurethane rubber is typically formed by mixing pre-polymer and curative agents to create a solid product, which is often used in applications like seals, gaskets, and linings. On the other hand, liquid system polyurethane rubber consists of a resin and curative that are mixed together to form a liquid material, which is then poured or sprayed onto a surface to create a protective coating or lining.</p></p>
