<p><p>The Low Noise Synthesizer market comprises various modulation types, including Frequency Modulation (FM), Phase Modulation (PM), and Pulse Modulation (PWM). FM synthesizers create rich sounds by varying the frequency of a carrier wave, resulting in complex waveforms. PM alters the phase of the carrier wave, creating unique audio textures. PWM uses pulse signals to modulate the width of narrow pulses, producing a range of tonal characteristics. These modulation techniques cater to diverse applications in electronic music and communication systems.</p></p>
