<p><p>Enteric capsules come in three main types - Hydroxypropyl Methylcellulose (HPMC), gelatin, and vegetable. HPMC capsules are made from cellulose and are suitable for vegetarian and vegan consumers. Gelatin capsules are derived from animal collagen and are commonly used in pharmaceutical products. Vegetable capsules are made from plant-based materials such as cellulose, making them suitable for consumers with dietary restrictions. Each type of enteric capsule offers different benefits and provides options for a variety of consumers in the market.</p></p>
