<p><strong>The Handheld Fiber Fusion Splicer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cladding Alignment</li><li>Core Alignment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld fiber fusion splicer market types include cladding alignment and core alignment splicers. Cladding alignment splicers align the outer layers of the fibers for connection, ideal for multimode fibers. Core alignment splicers align the cores of the fibers, providing high precision for single-mode fibers. Cladding alignment splicers are typically faster and easier to use, while core alignment splicers offer better performance and lower insertion loss. Both types of splicers play a crucial role in ensuring efficient and reliable fiber optic connections.</p></p>
