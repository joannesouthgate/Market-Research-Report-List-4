<p><p>Tumor embolization market includes various types such as Trans-Arterial Embolization (TAE), Drug-Eluting Bead Chemoembolization (DEB-TACE), Radioembolization (RE), and others. TAE involves blocking the blood flow to the tumor, DEB-TACE delivers chemotherapy drugs directly to the tumor site using tiny beads, and RE uses radioactive microspheres to target and kill cancer cells. Other methods in this market may include bland embolization and particle embolization, which are also used for treating tumors by cutting off their blood supply.</p></p>
