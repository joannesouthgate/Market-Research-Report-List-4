<p><p>Vacuum boosters are classified into three main types based on the technology used: oil-sealed pump, water-ring pump, and dry vacuum pump. Oil-sealed pumps use oil as a sealing and lubricating medium, water-ring pumps use water to create a vacuum, and dry vacuum pumps operate without any lubricant or sealing fluid. Each type has its own advantages and applications, making them suitable for various industries and processes that require vacuum technology.</p></p>
