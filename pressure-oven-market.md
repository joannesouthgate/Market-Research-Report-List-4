<p><p>Pressure Oven Market Types are categorised as Pressure Defoaming and Vacuum Pressure Defoaming. In Pressure Defoaming, the pressure inside the oven is increased to remove bubbles from the food being cooked, resulting in quicker and more even cooking. On the other hand, Vacuum Pressure Defoaming involves creating a vacuum inside the oven to remove air bubbles from the food, leading to improved texture and taste. Both methods are used in the market to enhance the cooking process and quality of the food.</p></p>
