<p><ul><li>Integral Angle Heads</li><li>Modular Angle Heads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Angle heads are used in machining operations to enable tools to reach difficult-to-access areas. There are two main types of angle heads in the market - Integral Angle Heads and Modular Angle Heads. Integral Angle Heads are compact units that are permanently fixed to the machine spindle, providing high rigidity and accuracy. On the other hand, Modular Angle Heads consist of interchangeable modules that allow for greater flexibility and versatility in tooling setups. Both types cater to different machining needs and offer unique advantages based on the application requirements.</p></p>
