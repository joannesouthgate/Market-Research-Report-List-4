<p><p>A vacuum relay is an electrical relay that uses a vacuum as the dielectric medium between the moving and fixed contacts. It offers various advantages such as high insulation resistance, low contact resistance, and very fast switching times. Vacuum relays are typically used in applications where high reliability and fast switching speed are required, such as in radio frequency (RF) and microwave applications, as well as in high voltage and high current applications.</p><p>The vacuum relay market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.40% during the forecast period. <p><p>The Vacuum Relay market consists of various types based on the number of poles and throws. DPDT (Double Pole Double Throw) relays have two sets of contacts that can switch between two separate circuits. SPDT (Single Pole Double Throw) relays have one set of contacts that can switch between two circuits. SPST (Single Pole Single Throw) relays have one set of contacts that can switch between two terminals. Each type of relay is designed for specific applications requiring different levels of switching capability.</p></p>
