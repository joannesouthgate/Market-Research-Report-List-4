<p><strong>The Hybrid Supercapacitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double Layer Type</li><li>Pseudocapacitive Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hybrid supercapacitors are classified into three main types: Double Layer Type, Pseudocapacitive Type, and Others. Double Layer Type supercapacitors store energy through the electrostatic separation of charge, offering high power density but lower energy density. Pseudocapacitive Type supercapacitors use reversible faradaic redox reactions to store energy, providing higher energy density compared to double layer capacitors. The Others market includes hybrid supercapacitors that utilize different energy storage mechanisms or materials, offering unique advantages for specific applications.</p></p>
