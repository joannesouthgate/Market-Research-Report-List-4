<p><ul><li>StrutLandingGear</li><li>RockerLandingGear</li><li>FramedLandingGear</li><li>PontoonLandingGear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Landing gear is essential component of an aircraft that helps in safe take-off and landing. There are four main types of landing gear in the market. Strut landing gear uses struts for support and shock absorption. Rocker landing gear utilizes a rocker arm mechanism for smooth landing. Framed landing gear consists of a sturdy frame structure for stability. Pontoon landing gear uses pontoon floats for landing on water. Each type of landing gear serves a specific purpose and is chosen based on the aircraft's design and intended use.</p></p>
