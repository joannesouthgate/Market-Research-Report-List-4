<p><p>An oil filled electric heater is a type of space heater that uses oil as a heat transfer fluid to generate heat. The oil is sealed inside the heater, where it is heated by an electric heating element. The heat is then transferred to the outer surface of the heater, which warms up the surrounding air.</p><p>The oil filled electric heater market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for energy-efficient heating solutions, especially in residential and commercial spaces. Oil filled electric heaters are known for their energy efficiency, as they provide consistent and even heat distribution. They are also considered safer than other types of space heaters, as the heating element is sealed and does not come into direct contact with the air.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the oil filled electric heater market include the integration of advanced safety features, such as overheat protection and tip-over switches. Manufacturers are also focusing on improving the design of oil filled electric heaters to make them more aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly. Additionally, the market is witnessing a rise in the adoption of smart oil filled electric heaters, which can be controlled remotely via smartphones or voice assistants.</p></p>
