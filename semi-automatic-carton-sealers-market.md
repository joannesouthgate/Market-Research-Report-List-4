<p><p>Semi-automatic carton sealers come in various types, including side belt driven, top and bottom belt driven, and others. Side belt driven sealers use side belts to drive cartons through the sealer for efficient sealing. Top and bottom belt driven sealers use belts on the top and bottom to securely seal cartons. Other types of sealers may include random carton sealers or high-speed sealers. Each type offers unique features and benefits depending on the specific needs of the packaging operation.</p></p>
