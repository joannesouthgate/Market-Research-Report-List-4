<p><ul><li>Immunoassay</li><li>Lateral Flow Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OTC tests market consists of two main types: immunoassay and lateral flow assay. Immunoassay tests involve detecting specific proteins in bodily fluids through antibodies. This type of test is commonly used for pregnancy tests and disease diagnosis. Lateral flow assay tests are simple, easy-to-use tests that provide quick results by detecting the presence of a target analyte in a sample. These tests are commonly used for infectious disease testing, drug testing, and food safety monitoring.</p></p>
