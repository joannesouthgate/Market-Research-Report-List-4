<p><p>LPWAN Asset Trackers utilize different market types such as Cellular LPWAN and LoRa. Cellular LPWAN technology operates on existing cellular networks, offering widespread coverage but may have higher power consumption. On the other hand, LoRa technology operates on unlicensed spectrum, providing long-range connectivity with low power consumption. Both technologies are commonly used for tracking assets in various industries due to their cost-effectiveness and efficiency in transmitting data over long distances. Customers can choose the type of LPWAN tracker based on their specific needs and priorities.</p></p>
