Molecular and Ionic Thin Film Memristors are based on the movement of ions or molecules in a thin film material to change resistance. Spin and Magnetic Memristors utilize magnetic properties to store and retrieve data. 3-terminal Memistors have an additional terminal for voltage control, allowing for more precise manipulation of resistance levels.
