<p><p>Flash point testing equipment is categorized into two main types: open cup flash point testers and closed cup flash point testers. Open cup flash point testers are designed with an open cup that is exposed to the air during testing, while closed cup flash point testers have a closed cup that prevents air from entering during testing. Both types of testers are essential for measuring the flash point of volatile liquids and are commonly used in industries such as oil and gas, chemicals, and manufacturing.</p></p>
