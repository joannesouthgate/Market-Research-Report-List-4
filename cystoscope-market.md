<p><p>Rigid cystoscopes are traditional devices used to examine the bladder walls and urinary tract. They are made of a rigid metal tube with a light source and lenses for visualization. On the other hand, flexible cystoscopes are newer, more advanced instruments that offer greater flexibility and maneuverability during procedures. They are made of a flexible fiber-optic tube with a camera at the tip, allowing for easier navigation through the urinary tract. Both types of cystoscopes are essential tools for diagnosing and treating various urinary tract conditions.</p></p>
