<p><p>The Labeler market is divided into two main types: Print & Apply Labeling and Labeling Equipment. Print & Apply Labeling involves printing labels on-demand and applying them to products or packaging automatically. This process is efficient and customizable, making it ideal for industries with diverse labeling needs. On the other hand, Labeling Equipment refers to standalone machines used for applying pre-printed labels onto products. These machines vary in complexity and can be manual or fully automated, catering to different production scales and requirements.</p></p>
