<p><strong>The HVAC Vacuum Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Double Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HVAC vacuum pumps are classified into two main types: single stage and double stage. Single stage vacuum pumps have one rotor and one set of vanes to create vacuum pressure, making them simpler and more affordable. Double stage vacuum pumps have two rotors and two sets of vanes, allowing for a higher level of vacuum pressure and better efficiency. Double stage pumps are often used in more demanding HVAC applications where a stronger vacuum is required.</p></p>
