<p><p>Electronic Grade Fiberglass Yarn is categorized into three main types based on their thickness: Thick (8-9 microns), Thin (5-7 microns), and Ultrathin (Below 5 microns). Thick yarn is known for its strength and durability, making it suitable for applications requiring high tensile strength. Thin yarn is used in applications where flexibility and tight weaving is important. Ultrathin yarn is used in delicate electronic components where precision and fine weaving is crucial. Each type caters to specific requirements in the electronics industry.</p></p>
