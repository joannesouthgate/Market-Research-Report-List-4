<p><p>Functional glass coatings encompass various types used to enhance glass performance. Pyrolytic coatings involve heating glass to apply a thin film, providing durability and thermal control. Sputtered coatings use a vacuum process to deposit metals or oxides, improving aesthetics and energy efficiency. Screen coatings involve applying patterns through a mesh stencil, often for decorative purposes. Spray coatings utilize aerosol technology for a uniform application of functional materials. Other methods may include sol-gel processes and organic coatings, each serving specific needs in construction and automotive sectors.</p></p>
